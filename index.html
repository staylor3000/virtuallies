About us

The people behind VirtuAllies

Get in touch

My name is Sam and I live in Poole in Dorset. I have worked within the financial services industry for over 34 years, and have gained extensive knowledge and understanding of how to support small businesses — particularly with an ever-changing regulatory regime and the additional time pressures this creates. I specialise in supporting Partners and Advisers of SJP with all areas of back-office support. Over the years I have developed a hands-on approach and am able to balance all the administrative and client servicing needs of a Practice. I have now taken the step to start my own business, offering my wealth of knowledge and skills within my new role as a virtual assistant.

I am supported by a colleague who has extensive experience within the civil service and a wealth of experience at executive level to support general business administration.

We provide a completely confidential, individually tailored service to each of our clients. Our time is charged by the hour and you can use our services as little or as often as your business requires. Whether you need us for a one-off project or for ongoing help, we will work to support you so you can concentrate on running your business.
